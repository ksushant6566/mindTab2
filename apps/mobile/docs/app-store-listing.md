## Description (4000 characters max)
MindTab is your professional workspace for tracking tasks, planning projects, and capturing notes — all in one clean, focused place.

**Set and track meaningful tasks**
Break down what matters into clear tasks. Organize them by project, track their status, and watch your progress unfold. Drag and drop to prioritize what's important right now.

**Capture everything**
Save articles, books, videos, podcasts, and websites as notes. Write quick thoughts or detailed note entries with the rich text editor. Everything is searchable and organized by project.

**Stay organized with projects**
Group your tasks and notes into projects to keep related work together. Switch between projects instantly with the project pills.

**Key features:**
- Task tracking with status management
- Rich text note-taking for articles, books, videos, podcasts, and websites
- Project-based organization
- Universal search across all your content
- Dark mode by default
- Google Sign-In for quick setup
- Syncs across devices

MindTab is built for people who want a single, distraction-free space to manage their personal tasks and knowledge.
