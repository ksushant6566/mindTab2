## Description (4000 characters max)
MindTab is your personal productivity companion for tracking goals, building habits, and capturing notes — all in one clean, focused workspace.

**Set and track meaningful goals**
Break down what matters into clear goals. Organize them by project, track their status, and watch your progress unfold. Drag and drop to prioritize what's important right now.

**Build lasting habits**
Create daily habits and track your streaks. See your week at a glance with the habit tracker grid, and stay motivated as your streak grows day by day.

**Capture everything**
Save articles, books, videos, podcasts, and websites as notes. Write quick thoughts or detailed journal entries with the rich text editor. Everything is searchable and organized by project.

**Stay organized with projects**
Group your goals and notes into projects to keep related work together. Switch between projects instantly with the project pills.

**Earn XP and level up**
Complete habits and goals to earn experience points. Level up as you make progress — a small reward system to keep you going.

**Key features:**
- Goal tracking with status management
- Daily habit tracking with streak counts
- Rich text note-taking for articles, books, videos, podcasts, and websites
- Project-based organization
- Universal search across all your content
- Dark mode by default
- Google Sign-In for quick setup
- Syncs across devices

MindTab is built for people who want a single, distraction-free space to manage their personal goals and knowledge.
